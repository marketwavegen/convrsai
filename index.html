HOW IT WORKS

Multiple Validation Layers. One Confident Score.

ConvrsAI ingests leads from any source and applies multiple validation and qualification layers. Every step removes uncertainty before it hits your sales motion.

01

Deep Web Validation

Validates each lead using deep web search and multiple data sources to verify company legitimacy, website presence, and industry classification

  • Company legitimacy
  • Website activity
  • Industry relevance
02

Firmographic Verification

Cross-checks and normalizes company size, revenue band, employee count, and growth indicators against your ICP rules.

  • Revenue matching
  • Employee verification
  • ICP alignment
03

Role & Title Validation

Validates job title accuracy, role seniority, and decision influence. Removes inflated, misleading, or irrelevant titles.

  • Seniority checks
  • Decision power
  • Department relevance
04

AI Email Qualification

Runs automated, intent-focused email conversations that ask qualification questions and detect readiness signals dynamically.

  • Intent detection
  • Dynamic follow-ups
  • Buying cues
05

AI Voice Qualification

Places short, AI-led voice calls to confirm interest, validate urgency, capture objections, and identify next steps.

  • Interest confirmation
  • Urgency validation
  • Objection capture
06

Conversation Intelligence

Converts every interaction into intent summaries, objection signals, timing indicators, and sales-ready context.

  • Intent summaries
  • Timing signals
  • Sales context
